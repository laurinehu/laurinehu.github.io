About Me

Laurine Huber

🕵️ Most of the time doing research
PhD Student @ LORIA, Nancy

💼 Sometimes teaching
Teacher @ IDMC, Nancy

💃 Rest of the time dancing and running 🏃

More precisely I’m a PhD student @ LORIA, University of Lorraine, France, working within Orpailleur team. Working among discourse and data mining, my work aims at considering the discourse or argumentation structure of the texts in the process of text mining. I’m interested in the representation of documents, especially by combining word or sentence embeddings produced by neural models and discourse structure annotated by humans.
